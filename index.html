Why import cars from the U.S.?

We compared the U.S. and European markets and realized that buying a car
in America is several times more profitable, no matter how paradoxical it may sound. This
is due to well thought-out logistics, the level of development of car assessment services
and the car buying process itself.

Most U.S. citizens lease a car for several years and all
the time the leasing company itself is engaged in a permanent
maintenance of the car, so cars from the U.S. - one of the best choices
for car enthusiasts in Ukraine.

Why is the price so low?

Broken cars from the U.S. are repurchased from insurance company auctions. At
these auctions a car loses half of its price even because of minimal
damage. If to take into consideration monetary expenses, namely redemption, delivery,
customs and repairs, the price for a car of the same condition in Ukraine
will be higher by 35-50%, and new cars will cost space money.
